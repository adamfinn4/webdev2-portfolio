This is the Gmail Page!
On this page I have information on what Gmail is, its purpose amd how to send an Email.

I have also included information about how a certain feature Gmail provides can help someone in the workplace or college environment.

Gmail is a free Web-based e-mail service currently being tested at Google that provides users with a gigabyte of storage for messages and provides the ability to search for specific messages. The Gmail program also automatically organizes successively related messages into a conversational thread.

How to sign up...

To start off making an account for Gmail you will need either a smartphone or a computer with internet access.
If you are on smartphone you'll need to go download the Gmail app from the appstore. If you're on computer you can visit gmail here!

Once you have downloaded the app and have opened it. You should be greeted with this screen.

In order to set up your gmail account:
-Just fill out the information form for your gmail account.
-And click "Next"

How to send an Email...

Gmails overall purpose as a program is to allow users to send emails to each other styled with their message along with files, documents, pictures or other important data that two (or more!) individuals need to talk about.
Here's how to send an email:
-First you will need to open the app on the homepage.
-From here select the compose button "+" at the bottom right of the screen.
-Select who you are sending the email to, add a subject and Compose your email.
-You can also add files to the email if you wish.

Useful Tip!

Gmail has distinguished istelf as the most popular emailing service and this is due to its simple design and hows it's full to the brim with features.
One of those features being scheduled emails. Sometimes it is best to wait for a specific time to email a company whether its for a job placement or an inquiry. Other times you just don't want to forget what you wanted to say. With the email scheduling feature you can compose your email whenever suits you and select a date and time for it to be emailed to its recipient(s).
Here's how to do that:
-First, click "+" to compose an email.
-Locate the stacked three dots in the upper right hand side.
-Tap the dots and select Schedule Send
-From here, just select the time and date you want it to send and tap save!
